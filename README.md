# The Choice Study (jsPsych)

A short online study examining how context influences decision-making in **Korean–American bicultural bilingual** adults.

**Live study:** https://neurochoice.github.io/identity-card-game/

## Overview
Participants complete a brief eligibility check, two short language sections (English and Korean), and a small set of interactive decision tasks. Typical completion time is **10–15 minutes**. Please complete the study until you see “Thank you” on the final screen; this confirms your responses were saved.

## Privacy
Responses are collected anonymously for research purposes.
